Dogs

Dogs are known for their loyalty and companionship. They come in various breeds, each with its own unique characteristics and traits. From playful puppies to wise old dogs, they bring joy to millions of households around the world.

Whether you're a fan of small breeds like the Chihuahua or large breeds like the Great Dane, there's a dog for every personality and lifestyle. Dogs make excellent friends, and their unconditional love is truly heartwarming.

A third of ALL households around the world have a dog. These playful, friendly, loyal animals make great companions, but they can also be fierce and tough protectors, or intelligent helpers.

Cats

Cats are mysterious and independent creatures. Known for their agility and grace, they are often considered as perfect companions for those who appreciate a more laid-back and low-maintenance pet.

From the playful antics of kittens to the regal demeanor of adult cats, feline friends have a way of capturing our hearts. Whether it's a fluffy Persian or a sleek Siamese, each cat brings its own charm and personality to the household.

Cats actually spend 70% of their lives sleeping, which works out to around 13-16 hours a day. It’s a cat’s life!
